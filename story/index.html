Summer 2021 - Star Play

Though they technically met over zoom screens in 2020 working on a digital theatre show for Dacha Theatre, their true in person meeting came with Star Play, one of Dacha's 2021 devised summer shows. Mimi was Sam's ride to rehearsal and they got to know each other through many a car conversation, scene partnership, and Spotify Blend.
