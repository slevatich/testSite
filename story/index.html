Star Play - Summer 2021

Though they technically met over zoom screens in 2020 working on a digital theatre show for Dacha Theatre, their true in person meeting came with Star Play, one of Dacha's 2021 devised summer shows. Mimi was Sam's ride to rehearsal and they got to know each other through many a car conversation, scene partnership, and Spotify Blend.

First Date - August 2021

No photos of our technical first date, which happened immediately after Star Play closed, but we did get this great photo taken of us at one of our early dates at the local Day In Day Out music festival, which has become one of our favorite yearly traditions.

Officially Dating: 9/12/21

After being pushed by some nosy friends on whether they were dating or not, and discussing the technicalities of the term, they decided, in fact, they were dating.

I love you... oops: 9/22/21

Instead of ending a coordinating phone call with their customary “I really really like you” message, Sam accidentally says what he's been thinking and ends a phone call with “love you”. The feeling, it turns out, is mutual.
