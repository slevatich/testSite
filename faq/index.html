Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I RSVP? Is this website even finished??

All great questions, though you don't have to be so rude with that second one! Now that invites are in the mail, all necessary features are present (namely the RSVP form) but we plan to add additional fun features until the wedding! At the end of the RSVP form there is a field where you can fill in your email to be updated on when we add something new. If you aren't sure if you are on the list there is no harm in adding that email again, you'll never get more than one email per update!

Are Plus Ones Allowed? What about kids?

All plus ones and kids welcome should be specifically noted on your invitation and show up in your attendees list when you RSVP through the form on the homepage. Number of kids is unfortunately limited due to wedding venue restrictions - we've had to be very precise! If you think we have made a mistake, please reach out!
