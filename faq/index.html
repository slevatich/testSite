Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I RSVP? Is this website even finished??

All great questions, though you don't have to be so rude with that second one! Now that invites are in the mail, all necessary features are present (namely the RSVP form) but we plan to add additional fun features until the wedding! At the end of the RSVP form there is a field where you can fill in your email to be updated on when we add something new. If you aren't sure if you are on the list there is no harm in adding that email again, you'll never get more than one email per update!

Are Plus Ones Allowed? What about kids?

All plus ones and kids welcome should be specifically noted on your invitation and show up in your attendees list when you RSVP through the form on the homepage. Number of kids is unfortunately limited due to wedding venue restrictions - we've had to be very precise! If you think we have made a mistake, please reach out!

What do I need to pack?

Aguascalientes in January is warm without being humid during the day (70s-80s) and gets rather chilly at night: as long as you pack a sweatshirt or coat for the evenings you should be well positioned! We recommend bringing a checked bag with some extra space in it if you can: you can bring back 1-2 bottles of nice tequila per person, we can point you to the right brands and spots to buy ;)

Are there any things I need to worry about with respect to diet?

The only thing you'll realistically need to worry about is eating too much because the food is too good! Jokes aside, there are no recommended pills or shots to get prior to travel, food safety is quite good as long as you aren't guzzling tap water, and Sam has not had any stomach issues. Its not too hard to get options that fit your dietary needs as well so we encourage arriving ready to eat well!
